About the Conference

The Green Jobs Career Fair & Conference is a premier event dedicated to sustainability, green career opportunities, and innovative solutions to environmental challenges. Organized by the Engineering Students Association (ESA) in collaboration with the Office of Career Services, this initiative aligns with the Chancellor‚Äôs Big 5 Agenda and aims to localize discussions from COP 29 (Conference of Parties) into grassroots efforts that drive real impact. Chancellor's Agenda

Professor Patrick Verkooijen is the current Chancellor of the University of Nairobi. He is the CEO of the Global Center on Adaptation, an international organization that works on climate change. The University of Nairobi's (UoN) Chancellor's agenda, known as the "Big 5," is a five-point plan to transform the university into a leader in education, technology, and entrepreneurship. The agenda includes initiatives to address challenges in Kenya, Africa, and the world, one of the key themes being Green Jobs: The university will work to create jobs in the renewable energy and sustainability sectors. The Green Jobs Career Fair & Conference is one of the initiatives that align with the Chancellor's vision.

About ESA

Our Mission

The Engineering Students Association (ESA) is a professional body of students from five departments in the School of Engineering, University of Nairobi.

Who We Are

ESA fosters collaboration, innovation, and professional development among students. Through workshops, events, and projects, we drive engineering excellence and sustainability.
